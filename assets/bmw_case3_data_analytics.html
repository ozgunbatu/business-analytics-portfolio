Actionable Insights & Business Impact

Insight 1: Geographic Performance Gap

Finding: Munich region converts 8.2% (web → sale) vs. Berlin 4.1%—despite similar traffic

Root Cause: Munich dealers respond to leads within 2 hours; Berlin dealers average 18 hours

Action: Implement lead routing SLA (2-hour response mandatory), reallocate €1.2M marketing budget from Berlin to Hamburg (high conversion, underinvested)

Impact: +180 annual sales (€7.2M revenue)

Insight 2: Test Drive Conversion Bottleneck

Finding: Only 55% of test drive requests actually happen (45% no-show/cancellation)

Root Cause: 72-hour avg wait time from request → scheduled drive (customers lose interest)

Action: Same-day/next-day test drive slots, automated SMS reminders 24h before

Impact: Test drive completion 55% → 75%, +250 annual sales

Insight 3: Finance Approval Friction

Finding: 28% of customers abandon after finance rejection (vs. 18% industry avg)

Root Cause: No alternative financing options offered, customers feel rejected

Action: Pre-qualify customers earlier (soft credit check), offer leasing/lower-tier options if rejected for purchase loan

Impact: Recover 40% of rejections, +95 annual sales
