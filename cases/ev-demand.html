EV Demand Forecast – Germany

SQL-driven demand forecasting to reduce over-stocking and optimize EV allocation.

Inventory −7%
Forecast error −22%
2.3M rows
SQL · Power BI

Problem Statement

Overstocking of EVs in some regions while others face shortage, leading to lost revenue and poor allocation efficiency.

Methodology

  • Extracted 2.3M sales & inventory records via SQL
  • Built Power BI dashboards to track monthly demand by region
  • Applied trend analysis and seasonal adjustments
  • Validated forecasts with historical data

Solution & Implementation

Developed a region-wise inventory allocation dashboard in Power BI and automated weekly SQL reports for operational teams.

Results / Impact

  • Inventory costs reduced by 7%
  • Forecast error reduced by 22%
  • Enhanced decision-making speed for operations team
